Colter Long, MEd RCC

Colter grew up on a farm outside of Stettler, Alberta. As a teenager, he was diagnosed with Leukemia and was the recipient of a bone marrow transplant. This experience influenced his interest in the biopsychosocial aspects of wellness. Colter is now a Registered Clinical Counsellor in Vancouver, BC.
